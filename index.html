Offenbach English Speakers

We are the friendly social group for Offenbach English speakers and those who would like to be. We are Americans, Mexicans, Polish, Iranian, German, etc. etc. and welcome all nationalities.

  • Join us Friday evenings in the summer at French wine and gin stand "Apres Midi" at Mathildenplatz
  • Join on Facebook: "Offenbach English speakers"
  • Come play cards with us Mondays (Schafkopf at Le Belge) and Tuesdays (social 66 at Bogside)
  • We occasionally host a local pub or kiosk crawl.
  • We participate in the monthly Offenbach AI Breakfast at Café Ziel
  • And in general aim to have a good time.
  • Many events are listed here: Meetup Group
